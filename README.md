# Discord-Bot

## ✨ Wonder of Everything: The Ultimate Server Companion

Bring a touch of magic and a mountain of utility to your Discord community with **Wonder of Everything**. Designed to be the "Swiss Army Knife" of bots, it balances high-level security with a whimsical personality that keeps your members engaged.

---

### 🛡️ Robust Admin & Moderation
Keep your server safe without breaking a sweat. Wonder of Everything provides a suite of professional tools to help moderators maintain order.

* **Smart Logging:** Detailed logs for deleted messages, edited content, and member updates so nothing slips through the cracks.
* **Auto-Mod Shield:** Customizable filters for links, spam, and blacklisted words to keep the environment clean 24/7.
* **One-Command Actions:** Swiftly kick, ban, or timeout users with clean, embed-style notifications sent directly to their DMs.
* **Role Management:** Seamlessly assign, remove, or create self-assignable roles for your community.

### 🛠️ Essential Utilities
Efficiency is at the core of the Wonder. These tools are designed to make server management a breeze.

* **Server Snapshots:** Get instant data on member counts, server age, and boost status.
* **Reminder System:** Set personal or channel-wide reminders for events or deadlines.
* **Poll Generator:** Create beautiful, reaction-based polls to gather community feedback in seconds.
* **User Look-up:** Check account creation dates and join dates to identify potential "alt" accounts or bot raids.

### 🎲 Fun & Interaction
A server shouldn't just be organized; it should be alive! Wonder of Everything brings the "wonder" back to your text channels.

* **Dynamic Fun Messages:** Randomly triggered easter eggs, witty comebacks, and "quote of the day" features.
* **Interactive Games:** Quick-fire trivia, coin flips, and "8-ball" style responses to keep the chat moving.
* **Economy Lite:** A simple, low-maintenance currency system that allows users to earn "Wonder Points" just for being active.
* **Leveling System:** Reward your most loyal members with customizable level-up messages and role rewards.

---

> **"Where Utility Meets Imagination."** > Whether you're running a professional gaming league or a cozy hangout spot, **Wonder of Everything** adapts to your needs, ensuring your server isn't just managed—it's marvelous.
